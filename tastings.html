Saturday Ritual

One tea at a time, together.

Every Saturday, Sophie guides a live virtual tasting — one tea, explored in depth. It's the single best way to accelerate your understanding and build the habit of tea as ritual.

What to Expect

A guided tasting, not a lecture.

Each session focuses on a single tea. Sophie walks you through the dry leaf, the aroma, the first steep, the second steep — teaching you to identify what you're tasting and why it matters. You'll leave with a vocabulary and a palate you didn't have before.

Sessions happen on Zoom every Saturday. All you need is the tea (available for purchase beforehand), hot water, and a vessel. We handle the rest.

How It Works

Three simple steps

No experience required. No equipment beyond what you already have. Just show up curious.

01

Get the tea

We announce each week's tea in advance. Order it from our shop or use what you have — we'll tell you what you need.

02

Join on Saturday

Hop on Zoom with your tea, hot water, and a cup. Sophie guides the session — you taste along in real time.

03

Keep the practice going

After the tasting, you'll have notes, vocabulary, and a new tea in your rotation. Come back next Saturday or explore on your own.
