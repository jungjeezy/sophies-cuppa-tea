What Membership Opens

The trip, the table, the judgment behind the tea.

The value of membership is not only the tea that arrives. It is access to years of tasting, sourcing trips, farm relationships, brewing judgment, and quiet correction that help a customer understand what they are drinking.

For members, Sophie’s becomes less like a catalog and more like a guided path: what to drink now, what to notice, what to revisit, and when a rare small lot is worth moving on quickly.
