Why Sophie's

This is not a tea catalog. It is a collection with judgment behind it.

Sophie's is built around teas chosen by people who drink, study, source, and teach this tea themselves. The point is not to make tea feel precious for its own sake. The point is to help customers taste the difference between anonymous commodity tea and a leaf selected through years of relationships, regional knowledge, processing judgment, and brewing practice.

01Clean sourcing

Tea selected with close attention to farming practices, purity, and the people responsible for the finished leaf.

02Human judgment

Turn a 400+ tea catalog into clear entry points: featured teas, brewing help, and membership for deeper learning.

03Cultural specificity

Teach Chinese tea through real context, regions, processing, and relationships instead of flattening it into generic wellness.
