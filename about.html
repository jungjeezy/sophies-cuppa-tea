Our Story

Not just premium tea.
A bridge into connoisseurship.

Thirty years in China's tea gardens.

Every tea we sell comes from a single farmer or processor — someone Sophie knows by name, has visited in person, and has worked with for years. No brokers. No importers. No blended supply chains. When you buy a tea from us, you know exactly who made it and where.

This isn't how most tea companies work. Most source through brokers, blend from multiple suppliers, or buy at auction. We go to the gardens, taste at the source, and bring back only what meets our standards. Thirty years of doing this means we have relationships that simply can't be replicated.

We don't just sell tea. We teach it. Our tastings, guides, and product pages are designed to help you cross the distance between curiosity and real understanding — without intimidation, and without dumbing anything down.
